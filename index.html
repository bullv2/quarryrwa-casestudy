How It Works: Under the Hood

Think of RWA as an "Automatic Trust Machine." We take the physical asset and plug it into a digital infrastructure.

Asset Verification

Paxos Gold: Instant Commodity Liquidity

Problem: Gold bars are heavy, expensive to move, and hard to split. Trading "paper gold" doesn't provide the same security as physical ownership.

Result: $400M+ in market value. Investors can trade $10 worth of real physical gold instantly on their phones at any time of day.
