Tokenising Real-World Assets on Chain

A case study on how Quarry bridges traditional asset ownership with decentralised infrastructure for transparent, fractional, and liquid investments.

Read the Case Study Explore Features

Overview

Quarry RWA enables the tokenisation of real-world assets — from real estate to commodities — unlocking liquidity and global access through blockchain technology.

The Problem

Traditional real-world assets are illiquid, geographically restricted, and require high minimum investment, locking out most retail participants.

The Solution

Quarry tokenises ownership into fractional, on-chain units — enabling 24/7 trading, transparent provenance, and programmable compliance.

The Outcome

Broader access, reduced settlement times, lower transaction costs, and a verifiable audit trail for every asset lifecycle event.

Key Features

Built for institutions and retail investors alike, with security and regulatory compliance at the core.

Fractional Ownership

Invest in high-value assets with as little as $10 through tokenised shares represented on-chain.

On-Chain Compliance

Smart-contract-enforced KYC/AML and jurisdiction rules ensure every transfer meets regulatory requirements.

Transparent Valuation

Oracle-fed pricing and on-chain attestation provide real-time, tamper-proof asset valuations.

Instant Settlement

Eliminate T+2 delays. Peer-to-peer transfers settle in seconds with finality on-chain.

Yield Distribution

Automated dividend and rental-yield payouts directly to token holders via smart contracts.

Multi-Chain Ready

Deployed across EVM-compatible chains with bridging support for cross-chain liquidity.
